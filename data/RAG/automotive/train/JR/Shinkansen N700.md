# Shinkansen N700 Series

The N700 series is a Japanese Shinkansen high-speed train with tilting capability developed jointly by JR Central and JR West.

## Variants

### 1. N700 (Original)
* **Features:** Introduced in 2007 to serve the Tokaido and Sanyo Shinkansen lines.

### 2. N700A (Advanced)
* **Features:** Features upgraded braking systems and better energy efficiency.

### 3. N700S (Supreme)
* **Features:** Debuted in 2020 with active suspension, improved aerodynamics, and an onboard battery system for emergencies.

## Status
The standard workhorse of Japan's most heavily trafficked high-speed rail corridor linking Tokyo and Osaka.
