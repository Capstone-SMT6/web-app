# Volvo FH

The Volvo FH is a heavy truck range manufactured by the Swedish company Volvo Trucks, introduced in 1993.

## Variants

### 1. FH16
* **Features:** The flagship model, featuring a monstrous 16-liter engine producing over 700 horsepower in recent variants. Overwhelmingly used for heavy haulage.

### 2. FH Electric
* **Features:** A relatively new zero-emission variant aimed at regional transport and city construction.

## Status
One of the most successful trucks in the world, renowned for its safety, driver comfort, and powertrain efficiency.
