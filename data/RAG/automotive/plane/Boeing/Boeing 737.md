# Boeing 737

The Boeing 737 is a narrow-body aircraft produced by Boeing at its Renton Factory in Washington.

## Variants

### 1. Original (100/200)
* **Era:** Launched in the late 1960s, featuring low-bypass turbofan engines.

### 2. Classic & Next Generation (NG)
* **Era:** From the 1980s to the 2010s, introducing modern engines and glass cockpits. The NG series (700/800/900) remains incredibly popular.

### 3. 737 MAX
* **Era:** The fourth and latest generation, featuring more efficient CFM LEAP engines and updated aerodynamics.

## Status
One of the best-selling commercial jetliners in history, forming the backbone of countless global airlines, especially low-cost carriers.
