# Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is a series of American automobiles manufactured by Ford. In continuous production since 1964, the Mustang is currently the longest-produced Ford car nameplate.

## Variants

### 1. EcoBoost
* **Engine:** Powered by a turbocharged 4-cylinder engine.
* **Reputation:** Offers an entry-level sports car experience with better fuel economy while retaining the iconic styling.

### 2. GT
* **Engine:** Equipped with a naturally aspirated V8 (traditionally the 5.0L Coyote engine).
* **Reputation:** The classic muscle car configuration, revered for its aggressive exhaust note and high horsepower.

### 3. Mach-E
* **Features:** A controversial departure from tradition, this is a fully electric compact crossover SUV that bears the Mustang name and styling cues.

## Status
Presently in its seventh generation (S650), it remains one of the most recognized and culturally significant vehicles in the world.
