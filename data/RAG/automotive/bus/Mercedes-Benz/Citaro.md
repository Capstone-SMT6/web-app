# Mercedes-Benz Citaro

The Mercedes-Benz Citaro is a highly popular single-deck rigid or articulated bus manufactured by Mercedes-Benz/EvoBus, introduced in 1997.

## Variants

### 1. Urban Variant
* **Setup:** Typically features a low floor for easy boarding.
* **Performance:** Powered by diesel, CNG, or more recently, hybrid and electric powertrains.
* **Reputation:** The staple of European public transportation networks.

## Status
Currently in its second generation (C2), widely deployed globally with an increasing focus on the zero-emission eCitaro models.
