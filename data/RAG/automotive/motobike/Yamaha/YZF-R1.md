# Yamaha YZF-R1

The Yamaha YZF-R1, or simply R1, is a 998 cc sports motorcycle made by Yamaha since 1998.

## Variants

### 1. Standard R1
* **Engine:** Features a crossplane crankshaft inline-four engine, delivering smooth, linear torque.
* **Reputation:** Renowned for its aggressive styling and track-focused performance.

### 2. R1M
* **Features:** A higher-spec track version including electronic suspension, carbon fiber bodywork, and data logging capabilities.

## Status
A legendary liter-bike that continues to compete at the highest levels in the Superbike World Championship (WSBK).
