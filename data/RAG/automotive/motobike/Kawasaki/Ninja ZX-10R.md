# Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R is a motorcycle in the Ninja sport bike series from the Japanese manufacturer Kawasaki, the successor to the Ninja ZX-9R. It was originally released in 2004.

## Variants

### 1. Standard ZX-10R
* **Engine:** A 998cc inline-four engine known for its extremely high-revving nature.
* **Features:** Advanced aerodynamics and a comprehensive electronics suite including traction control and launch control.

### 2. ZX-10RR
* **Features:** A limited-production homologation special designed explicitly for Superbike racing, featuring lightweight titanium engine internals and forged wheels.

## Status
Highly successful in motorsport, winning numerous Superbike World Championship (WSBK) titles, heavily associated with rider Jonathan Rea.
