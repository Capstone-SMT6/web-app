# Samsung Galaxy S24

The Samsung Galaxy S24 is a series of premium Android smartphones designed, developed, and marketed by Samsung Electronics as part of its Galaxy S series.

## Variants

### 1. Base S24 & S24+
* **Features:** Offer flagship performance with versatile triple-camera setups. The "+" model offers a larger battery and screen.

### 2. S24 Ultra
* **Features:** The highest-end model, featuring a squarer design, a built-in S-Pen stylus, a titanium frame, and arguably the best zoom camera system on a smartphone.

## Status
Released in early 2024, heavily marketing "Galaxy AI" features like live translation and advanced photo editing.
