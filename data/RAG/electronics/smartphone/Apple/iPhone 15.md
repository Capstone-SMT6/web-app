# Apple iPhone 15

The iPhone 15 is a line of smartphones designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc., representing the 17th generation of iPhones.

## Variants

### 1. iPhone 15 & 15 Plus
* **Features:** The standard models featuring an aluminum frame, the introduction of the Dynamic Island to the base lineup, and the heavily requested transition to a USB-C port.

### 2. iPhone 15 Pro & Pro Max
* **Features:** Premium models featuring a new, lighter titanium chassis, the powerful A17 Pro chip (capable of hardware-accelerated ray tracing), and advanced camera systems with improved optical zoom.

## Status
Released in September 2023, widely adopted for its ecosystem integration and the standardization of the USB-C charging port.
