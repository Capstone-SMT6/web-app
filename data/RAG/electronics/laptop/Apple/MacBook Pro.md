# MacBook Pro

The MacBook Pro is a line of high-end Macintosh portable computers introduced by Apple in 2006.

## Variants

### 1. 14-inch MacBook Pro
* **Features:** Features an Apple Silicon chip (M-series), a Liquid Retina XDR display, and the return of popular ports like MagSafe, HDMI, and an SD card reader.

### 2. 16-inch MacBook Pro
* **Features:** The largest model, aimed at power users needing maximum screen real estate and thermal headroom for intense tasks like video rendering.

## Status
The transition to Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3 chips) has revitalized the lineup, offering unparalleled performance-per-watt in the laptop space.
