# AMD Ryzen 5

AMD Ryzen 5 is a mid-range family of x86-64 microprocessors introduced by AMD in 2017, based on the Zen microarchitecture.

## Variants

### 1. Desktop Processors (e.g., Ryzen 5 7600X)
* **Performance:** Typically offers 6 cores and 12 threads. Excellent for gaming and general productivity.

### 2. Mobile/APU Processors (e.g., Ryzen 5 7500U)
* **Performance:** Tailored for laptops with lower power draw and integrated Radeon graphics.

## Status
Extremely popular among budget/mid-range PC builders, consistently challenging Intel's Core i5 lineup in value and performance.
