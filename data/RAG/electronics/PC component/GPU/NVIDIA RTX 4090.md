# NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090

The GeForce RTX 4090 is an ultra-high-end desktop graphics processing unit (GPU) produced by NVIDIA, based on the Ada Lovelace architecture.

## Variants

### 1. Founders Edition
* **Features:** Produced directly by NVIDIA, featuring a massive and intricate cooling solution, known for its premium aesthetic and very high build quality.

### 2. AIB Partner Cards
* **Features:** Manufactured by third-party companies (e.g., ASUS, MSI, Gigabyte) featuring custom cooling solutions, factory overclocks, and aggressive, often RGB-heavy designs.

## Status
Currently holds the undisputed title of the fastest consumer graphics card on the market, particularly excelling in 4K high-refresh-rate gaming and heavy 3D rendering tasks.
