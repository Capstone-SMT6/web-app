# Logitech G502

The Logitech G502 is a highly popular wired/wireless gaming mouse manufactured by Logitech G.

## Variants

### 1. G502 HERO
* **Features:** The classic wired version featuring Logitech's highly accurate HERO sensor and adjustable weights.

### 2. G502 LIGHTSPEED
* **Features:** The wireless variant, utilizing Logitech's ultra-low latency wireless technology.

### 3. G502 X
* **Features:** A modern redesign with optical-mechanical switches, a lighter chassis, and an arguably cleaner aesthetic.

## Status
Widely considered one of the best-selling and most beloved gaming mice of the modern era.
