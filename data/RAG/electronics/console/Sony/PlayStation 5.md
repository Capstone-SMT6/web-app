# PlayStation 5

The PlayStation 5 (PS5) is a home video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment, launched in November 2020.

## Variants

### 1. Standard Edition
* **Features:** Includes a custom AMD Zen 2 CPU, RDNA 2 GPU, an ultra-fast custom NVMe SSD, and an Ultra HD Blu-ray disc drive.

### 2. Digital Edition
* **Features:** Identical in performance to the standard model, but excludes the physical disc drive for a slightly lower price point.

### 3. PS5 Slim & Pro
* **Features:** Sony recently updated the lineup with a slimmer chassis, and rumors/leaks constantly surround an upcoming Pro model (PS5 Pro) for enhanced performance.

## Status
A wildly successful "ninth-generation" console, famous for its DualSense controller's haptic feedback and exclusive game library.
