# Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is a video game console developed by Nintendo and released worldwide in most regions on March 3, 2017. 

## Variants

### 1. Standard Switch
* **Features:** A hybrid console that can be played natively using its built-in LCD screen or docked to play on a larger television. Features detachable Joy-Con controllers.

### 2. Switch Lite
* **Features:** A purely handheld, cheaper variant. The controllers are permanently attached, and it cannot output video to a TV.

### 3. Switch OLED Model
* **Features:** An updated version of the standard model featuring a larger, much more vibrant OLED screen, a better kickstand, and enhanced audio.

## Status
One of the best-selling consoles of all time, largely driven by exclusive mega-hits like *The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild* and *Mario Kart 8 Deluxe*.
